⚽ Penalty Kings — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 June 2026

This app collects no data. None. Everything stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Penalty Kings does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. It has no user accounts, no sign-in, no ads, no analytics, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

Your game data

Your tournament history and career statistics are saved only on your own device (in the app's local storage). This data never leaves your device, is never sent to us or anyone else, and is removed if you delete the app or clear its data from within the game.

Network & third parties

The app is fully offline and makes no network requests. No data is shared with third parties because no data is collected in the first place.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data at all, it collects no data from children. It is suitable for all ages.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
