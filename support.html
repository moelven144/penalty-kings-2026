How to play

Pick one of the 48 nations and win every match on penalties — from the group stage all the way to the final. You take the kicks and dive in goal.

ShootingTap (or press SPACE) to lock direction, then again to lock power. Aim for the corners.
KeepingTap a goal zone — or use the keys Q W E (top) / A S D (low) — to dive.

Tip: the match plays great in landscape on phones. Your tournament history and career stats are saved on your device.

Frequently asked

Is it free / online? Yes, it's free and works fully offline — no account, no ads, no data collection (see our Privacy Policy).

Lost my stats? Stats live only on your device. Deleting the app or using “Clear all data” in Career & History removes them.
